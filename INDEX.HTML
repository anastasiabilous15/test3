FIVE LARGEST PROTESTS IN U.S. HISTORY

The tradition of the protests that demand progressive change in the United States continue.

Here are the country's five of the top protests in terms of attendance:

  1. George Floyd protests nationwide in 2020 with an estimated 26 million protestors.
  2. 2017 Women's March in 2017 drew about 5.6 million people nationwide.
  3. March for Our Lives a student led protest in 2018 against gun violence, drew about 2 million people.
  4. 2018 Women's March in 2018 drew about 1.5 million protestors.
  5. March on Washington for Lesbian, Gay and Bi Equal Rights and Liberation drew 1 million people in 1993.
